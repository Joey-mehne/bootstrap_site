Top 5 Best Weapons in Dead Cells

Weapons ranked from worst to best

  1. Cursed Sword
    • The Cursed Sword does 554 base damage which is more than any weapon in the game.
    • You will die in one hit no matter what you do.
    • Too much of a risk to be any good.
  2. Spartan Sandals
    • The Spartan Sandals do a pitiful 32 base damage on their own.
    • The only way to do any bonus dase is to kick enemies into a wall which will not always be available.
    • Only grab if incredibly desperate.
  3. Tentacle
    • The Tentacle does 79 base damage which isn't a lot but will score criticals if you press the attack button again after sticking to an enemy.
    • This weapon allows you to latch on to enemies and wall in order to bring you closer to them.
    • Better used for increased mobility rather than a weapon.
  4. Symmetrical Lance
    • The Symmetrical Lance does 194 base which is definitely one of the more powerful weapons in the whole game.
    • This weapon does critical hits for 6 seconds after you kill 2 enemies in a row which then does 288 base damage.
    • This also does damage on both side of you so this is a pretty good weapon for later on in the game.
  5. Shovel
    • THIS IS THE BEST WEAPON IN THE ENTIRE GAME DOING 999999999999 DAMAGE AT ALL TIMES!
    • YOU CAN SMACK BOMBS BACK AT ENEMIES TO GIVE THEM A TASTE OF SWEET REVENGE!
    • GOT ENEMIES SURROUNDING YOU? NO PROBLEM! GIVE THEM THE SWIFT BOP TO THE FACE AND SEND THEM FLYING!
    • TAKE THIS IF YOU ABSOLUTELY WANT TO DESTROY YOUR ENEMIES AND MAKE SURE THEY WISH THEY NEVER PICKED A FIGHT WITH YOUR GREATNESS!
